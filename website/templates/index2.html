Quasill

From Symptoms to Clarity

Quasill is a user-friendly app designed to help you understand your symptoms better. It provides a list of possible diseases associated with your symptoms, all by leveraging the power of artificial intelligence. Our aim is to offer an insightful, AI-driven diagnosis to guide your health decisions.
